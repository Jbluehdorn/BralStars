**External Goal:**
Cold hard cash. Also shiny things. Usually strikes a balance between money drive and self-preservation — but there's a specific **ultra-rare diamond** she has convinced herself is the ultimate heist. The one that will set her up for life.

**Internal Conflict:**
Han-Solo mode. Has cut herself off from all human attachment and personal loyalty. Only loyal to herself, cash, and shiny things. Any bond that forms is a threat to the person she thinks she has to be.

**Threads for the DM:**
- The diamond — where is it, who has it, what does chasing it cost her?
- What breaks the shell? A crew? A cause? A debt she can't buy out of?
- Kenku voices as flavor at the table.
