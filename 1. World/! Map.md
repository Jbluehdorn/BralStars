# World

## World Facts (Session 0 canon)
- **Barter economy by weight** — coin, gems, and any goods, all measured by scale.
- **The center of the Rock is hollow** — old mining, ancient illithid warrens beneath. See [[The Tunnels]].
- **The magical gravity generator is failing** — people are falling off. Rumor: it's the naval-base workers who maintain the fields who are dropping. See [[Rock of Bral]].
- **The Rock was once an illithid colony** — nobody remembers when. The [[Illithid Crucible]] is one very awake remnant.
- **Prince Andru has a clown secret police** — [[The Secret Service Clowns]], led by [[Ronald McDonald]].
- **There is something very secret at the center of the hedge maze at [[Starhaven]].**
