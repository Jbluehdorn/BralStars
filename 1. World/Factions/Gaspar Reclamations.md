**Description:**
Everyone thinks Gaspar Reclamations is a front for the [[The Donjon Crime Family|Donjon Crime Family]]. Everyone is wrong. It is genuinely a mom-and-pop scrap operation run by *very nice people* who are baffled by their own reputation.

**The catch:**
They are feeding the scrap to a **giant monster** in the back yard. It is their pet. They love it very much. They do not consider this notable enough to advertise.

**Who fuels the rumor:**
[[Mafia Don Jon]] himself. It gives him a plausible-deniability decoy for the actual Donjon operations. The Gaspars have no idea.

**Adventure hooks:**
- Something a party needs was scrapped last week — and eaten last night.
- The pet outgrows the yard.
- Someone tries to shake down the Gaspars for "protection." The pet handles it.
