# Overview
**House Traken** is the dominant merchant noble house on the Rock of Bral. They control a significant portion of the import/export trade flowing through the docks, and their network of agents spans multiple crystal spheres.

House Traken maintains their power through legitimate trade, political marriages, and the occasional aggressive business deal. They are not above hiring adventurers to retrieve shipments, eliminate competition, or explore new trading opportunities.

# Members
- **Lord Traken** — patriarch, aging but sharp
- **Lady Sera Traken** — ambitious daughter, the real power behind the house's expansion plans

# Relations
- Rivals with other noble houses for trade dominance
- Uneasy truce with [[The Juggler's Guild]] — they know better than to step on each other's toes
- Cultivated relationship with [[The Arcane]] for access to spelljamming components
