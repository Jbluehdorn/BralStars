# Overview
**The Arcane** are a mysterious race of tall, blue-skinned humanoids who hold a near-monopoly on the manufacture and sale of **spelljamming helms** — the magical devices required to pilot a spelljamming vessel. They are found on almost every major trade hub across the known crystal spheres.

The Arcane are scrupulously neutral in all conflicts. They will sell to anyone — pirates, paladins, or princes — and they never discuss their own origins or where the helms are made. Attempts to reverse-engineer their helms always fail.

They communicate through intermediaries called **Arcane Factors**, who maintain offices on major ports.

# Known Prices (Approximate)
- Minor spelljamming helm: 25,000 gp
- Major spelljamming helm: 50,000 gp
- Helm maintenance/repair: negotiable

# Relations
- Neutral with all factions — by deliberate policy
