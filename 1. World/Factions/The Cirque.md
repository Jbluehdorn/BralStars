**Description:**
A legitimate circus troupe that would *very much* like to be seen as a legitimate circus troupe. They have a rich, well-earned history of being contract killers, and the Rock has not forgotten.

**The open secret:**
The fights they run in **[[The Arena|the Arena of Frun]] are scripted, WWE-style.** Fixed outcomes, choreographed spectacle. The crowd doesn't know (or pretends not to). Money on the fights is very real. Anyone who figures it out becomes a problem for the Cirque.

**The other open secret:**
Every member of the Cirque is *terrified of clowns.* Not the [[The Secret Service Clowns|Secret Service Clowns]] specifically — clowns in general. Nobody in the Cirque will explain why. Nobody in the Cirque *can*.

**Key figures:**
- [[Ringmaster Blorg]] — a crocodile in a ringmaster's outfit. Nothing else remarkable about him. Everyone respects him.
- [[Fairy Floss]] — former Cirque assassin, atoning by trying to make people happy. (Also trying to kill [[Ronald McDonald]].)

**Adventure hooks:**
- A fixed fight goes off-script — who broke the pact and why?
- The Cirque hires the party to find out what the clowns *are*.
- Fairy Floss's past catches up with the troupe.
