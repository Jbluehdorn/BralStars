**Description:**
A full-blown mafia operation. Made men, cell blocks, ledgers, passwords, a vault. All rats.

They were made sapient — language, memory, ambition, personality — by the [[Illithid Crucible]] humming away in [[Don Leto di Frommagio|Don Leto]]'s office. The imprinting also produced the parallel mouse population (see [[Charlee Chees]]) and, downstream, the imprinted insects of [[The Tunnels]] (see [[Manny]] & [[Ramirez]]).

**Roster:** the Don, his officers ([[Donny Knuckles Moreno|Donny "Knuckles"]] and [[Timothee Chalamet]]), the muscle ([[Tommy Lock-Lips Daluca|Tommy]], [[Jimmy The Tail Branzino|Jimmy]], [[Bobby Malone]]), the Slop crew ([[Billy Skeeter]], [[Joey Skeeter]], [[Johnny Jerk-Off Ramone|Johnny]], [[Slimey Rizzo]], [[Skinny Pete]]), and [[Pauley Love Boy Amaretto|Pauley]] & [[Mary Anne Susan Calivari di Frommagio|Mary Anne]].
