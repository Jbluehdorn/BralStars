**Description:**
Old-money organized crime operating out of an estate that everyone politely refers to as "the Donjon." Rackets, protection, cargo skimming at [[The Docks]], and a hand in most of the schemes the Rock's elites deny existing.

**Decoy operation:**
[[Mafia Don Jon]] himself seeds the rumor that [[Gaspar Reclamations]] is a Donjon front. It isn't. The rumor keeps investigators looking the wrong way while the actual Donjon business moves.

**Adventure hooks:**
- A job posted on Luigi's board is a Donjon setup.
- The Di Frommagios and the Donjons come into contact. Nobody expected the rats.
- A Gaspar innocent gets grabbed for a crime the Donjons committed.
