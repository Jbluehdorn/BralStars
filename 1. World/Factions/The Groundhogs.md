**Description:**
Revered guides who will take you through the hollowed tunnels and mines under the Rock. They do not speak — not to clients, not to each other, not to themselves. Payment is by gesture and by weight (see [[Rock of Bral]]).

**The catch:**
Sometimes they just… leave people. In the wrong place. There is no known appeals process. Rumor says the ones they leave behind end up at [[Valkan's Legion]].

**What they know:**
- Every route beneath the Rock, including the ways to [[Valkan's Legion]] and the old illithid warrens.
- Where the [[Rock of Bral|gravity anchor]] is failing.
- Who else is walking down there.

**Adventure hooks:**
- The party hires a Groundhog. Do they arrive?
- A Groundhog gestures for the party to *follow* — unpaid, uninvited.
- Someone is killing Groundhogs. In silence.
