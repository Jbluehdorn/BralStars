> ##### The Hammers

**Description:**
An intersectional community organized to redistribute wealth from the Rock's elites down to the neighborhoods that need it. Sincere. Ideological. Chronically bogged down in process.

**The catch:**
They have an **HOA** and it is *actively disputing* how to distribute what they take. Every heist ends in a three-hour meeting.

**Key figures:**
- [[Jennifer Coolidge]] — HOA president. Wants everything to look real nice. Inept.
- [[Kay Aren]] — Jennifer's assistant. Secretly wants to burn down the Boroughs and put up an apartment complex.

**Secrets & leverage:**
- They know [[Professor DC]]'s secret (he's the only real mage at the Mages' Hall) and **skim off the funds he embezzles.**

**Adventure hooks:**
- Party gets recruited to hit a High City target — proceeds tied up in HOA arbitration.
- Kay Aren's real plan surfaces. Do the Hammers eat their own?
