# Overview
**The Juggler's Guild** is the dominant criminal organization on the Rock of Bral, controlling smuggling, pickpocketing, fencing, and information brokering throughout the Low City. The identity of the guild's leader, known only as "The Juggler," is one of the Rock's best-kept secrets.

The Guild is pragmatic — they don't kill without reason and generally avoid attracting the attention of Prince Andru's constabulary. They see themselves as a parallel economy, providing services the legitimate market won't touch.

# Operations
- **Smuggling** — moving goods that bypass the docking tax
- **Information brokering** — buying and selling secrets
- **Fencing** — moving stolen goods
- **Muscle for hire** — for jobs that need deniability

# Relations
- Uneasy coexistence with [[House Traken]]
- Actively hostile to any adventurers who interfere with their operations
- The Guild has informants inside the constabulary
