**Description:**
A small group of pseudo-hippies who live at the edges of **[[Lake Bral]]** and worship the water itself. They are gentle, welcoming, and *unshakably certain* that the Lake is a god.

**Open questions:**
- Is the Lake a god?
- Is the Lake alive?
- Why has nobody who swam out past the third buoy come back?

**Adventure hooks:**
- Someone the party knows joins the Lovers and stops answering messages.
- The Lake wants a favor. It has ways of asking.
- The Lovers know something about the illithid past of the Rock (see [[Rock of Bral]]) they do not know they know.

*Every offering and every pilgrim nudges it closer to consciousness. When this fills, the Lake starts making requests nobody can refuse.*
