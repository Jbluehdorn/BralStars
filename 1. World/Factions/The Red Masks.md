**Description:**
A bardic house where performers gather to trade songs, share information, and find work. In practice: the finest rumor mill on the Rock. If it happened, a Red Mask heard about it. If it didn't happen, a Red Mask will sing about it until it does.

**The war:**
The [[The Secret Service Clowns|Secret Service Clowns]] kidnap Red Masks and convert them into clowns. The Red Masks know. They have not figured out what to *do* about it. Numbers are quietly dropping.

**Adventure hooks:**
- A Red Mask contact asks the party to check on a member who missed rehearsal.
- The Red Masks pay for intelligence on Starhaven's hedge maze.
- A song being sung on the Rock this week is *very* specific about a job the party did last week.
