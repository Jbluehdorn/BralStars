**Description:**
[[Prince Andru]]'s secret police. All clowns. Officially they do not exist. Unofficially they run a hedge-maze at Starhaven where dissidents get *chased* until they don't come back out.

**Recruitment:**
They kidnap [[The Red Masks]] and press-gang them into the ranks. Musicians and bards make good clowns, apparently. Nobody knows the conversion rate.

**Leadership:**
- [[Ronald McDonald]] — leader. Pure-white appearance he takes vengeance on others for. Immortal demigod. Reproduces asexually.

**Secrets & leverage:**
- Prince Andru is the founder of "McDonalds" (the org — or the man?). [[Steve]] the palace guard just found this out and is not okay.
- The Red Masks know they are being hunted. They also know the Clowns *know* they know.

**Adventure hooks:**
- A Red Mask friend of a PC vanishes. Where do the missing bards go?
- The party is invited (i.e. summoned) into the hedge maze.
- Someone in the Cirque wants Ronald McDonald dead. So does [[Fairy Floss]].
