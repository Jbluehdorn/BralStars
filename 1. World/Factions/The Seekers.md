# Overview
**The Seekers** are the informal adventurers' guild of the Rock of Bral. Unlike more formal guilds, the Seekers don't charge membership dues or require exclusive contracts — they simply provide a network of resources, information, and mutual aid for those brave (or foolish) enough to explore Wildspace.

The Seekers maintain the **Adventurers' Board** at [[The Laughing Beholder]], curating legitimate job postings and removing scams.

# Resources for Members
- Access to the Adventurers' Board
- Discounted healing potions from guild stock
- Maps of known explored regions
- Emergency rescue fund (for extracting stranded parties)

# Relations
- Good standing with [[Prince Andru]] — the Seekers do work the constabulary can't
- Friendly with [[Large Luigi]] — the Laughing Beholder is their de facto headquarters
- Neutral with noble houses — they work for coin, not politics
