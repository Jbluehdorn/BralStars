> | | |

**Description:**
Officially: a mining outfit working the hollowed veins beneath the Rock and the wider Realmspace. Unofficially: they don't just mine, they *erase* — sites, species, populations. The name is the tell.

**The CEO:**
Genuinely lovely. Deeply well-intentioned. Utterly out of touch with what her own company is doing three levels down the org chart. Would be horrified to learn the truth. Might not survive learning it.

**Secrets & leverage:**
- Whoever briefs the CEO controls the company.
- Their mining operations are probably compromising the hollow center of the Rock (see [[Rock of Bral]]).

**Adventure hooks:**
- A site the party visits has been *xenoterminated* since the maps were drawn.
- Someone wants the CEO given a very frank tour of a site.
- The failing gravity generator (see [[Rock of Bral]]) — is Xenotermination undermining the anchor?
