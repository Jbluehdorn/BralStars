> ##### The Illithid Crucible
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | Type | Alien artifact (illithid) |
> | Location | [[Don Leto di Frommagio]]'s office, Di Frommagio hideout |
> | Effect | Psionically imprints sapience on nearby vermin |

**Description:**
A giant purple gem, floor to ceiling — an *awesome* sight, especially if you happen to be the size of a rat. It sits in a base of strange alien metal, ringed with little dead tentacles that once interfaced with illithid machinery long since gone. The gem hums at the edge of hearing. It is very old, very wrong, and very much *awake.*

**Function:**
The Crucible is what has been quietly imprinting sapience — language, memory, ambition, *personality* — onto the rats and mice living in the tunnels beneath the Rock. The Di Frommagio crime family exists because of it. The mice faction exists because of it. Everything strange about the vermin under Bral traces back to this stone.

**Notes:**
- The rats revere it. They call the chamber "the room that hums" and most avoid it.
- The mice know something is wrong down here but not what.
- What the Di Frommagios plan to *do* with it is the campaign question.
