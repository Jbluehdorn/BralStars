**Description:**
A crocodile. In a ringmaster's outfit. Runs [[The Cirque]]. That's it. That's the entire pitch.

He does not speak — nothing supernatural about it, he's a crocodile. He gestures. He cracks a whip. He looms.

**The strange fact:**
Nobody knows *why* they respect him. Everybody does. Even people who've never seen the circus. Something about Blorg. Nobody can name it.

**Adventure hooks:**
- [[Warden Bigboots]] wants him skinned for shoes. Someone should probably warn Blorg.
- The Cirque's fear of clowns — Blorg *especially* — starts affecting his ability to run the venue.
- Somebody has to negotiate with Blorg. It falls to the party.
