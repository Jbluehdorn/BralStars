**Description:**
The Don. Head of the Di Frommagio crime family. Father of [[Mary Anne Susan Calivari di Frommagio]]. Currently deliberating what to do with [[Chester Chees]]. Keeps the [[Illithid Crucible]] in his office, flanked at all times by his two top officers, [[Donny Knuckles Moreno|Donny "Knuckles" Moreno]] and [[Timothee Chalamet]].

**In a fight:** Fights valiantly, but the moment he realizes he's in true mortal danger he escapes through the secret tunnel at the back of the fireplace in his office. His officers will die where they stand to buy him the seconds he needs.
