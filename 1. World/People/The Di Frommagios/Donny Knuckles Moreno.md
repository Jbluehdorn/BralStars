**Description:**
One of [[Don Leto di Frommagio]]'s two top officers. A slab of a rat, built like a barrel with fists to match. Speaks rarely, hits hard, and would take a bullet for the Don without blinking.

**Story:**
Donny is muscle first, loyalty second, thought third. He came up with Don Leto and considers the family the only thing worth dying for. If the Don is threatened, Donny will fight to the death — no negotiation, no surrender, no running.
