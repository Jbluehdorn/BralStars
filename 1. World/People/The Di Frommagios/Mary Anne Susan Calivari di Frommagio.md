**Description:**
Pauley's girlfriend. Here for the romance, not for the crime. Also — and this is the load-bearing detail — she is **the Don's daughter.** Anything that happens to her is a *very* big deal to the family.

**Story:**
Mary Anne and Pauley want *out* of the crime life. She loves Pauley more than she loves the family, and she'd leave the Rock with him tomorrow if it were survivable. It isn't. If they run, her father [[Don Leto di Frommagio]] will hunt Pauley down — the Don wouldn't hurt his daughter, but Pauley is a dead rat the moment he leaves. They'll never be safe. She hasn't told her father any of this.
