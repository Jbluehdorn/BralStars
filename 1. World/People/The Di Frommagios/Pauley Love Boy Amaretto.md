> ##### Pauley "Love Boy" Amaretto
> | | |
> |---|---|

> | Faction | Di Frommagio crime family |

**Description:**
Young, hopelessly in love, trying to have a romantic evening with [[Mary Anne Susan Calivari di Frommagio]]. Not thinking about work.

**Story:**
Pauley and Mary Anne want *out* of the crime life. They dream of running away together and starting a quiet life somewhere far from the family. The problem: if they run, [[Don Leto di Frommagio]] will hunt Pauley down to the ends of Wildspace. There is no version of "running" that ends with them safe. Pauley knows it. Mary Anne knows it. Neither has figured out what to do about it yet.
