**Description:**
One of [[Don Leto di Frommagio]]'s two top officers. A skinny, sharp-cheeked rat with a menacing quiet about him — the kind of quiet that comes right before something bad happens. Wields magic that shouldn't come naturally to a rodent, and enjoys the fact that it doesn't.

**Story:**
Timothee is the brains-and-magic counterweight to [[Donny Knuckles Moreno|Donny "Knuckles"]]'s muscle. Where Donny hits, Timothee hexes. Fanatically loyal to the Don and will fight to the death to protect him.
