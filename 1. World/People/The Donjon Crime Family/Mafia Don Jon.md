**Description:**
Head of the Donjon Crime Family. Operates from an old estate everyone politely calls "the Donjon."

**Signature scheme:**
He is personally responsible for the persistent rumor that [[Gaspar Reclamations]] is a mob front. It isn't — they are a lovely family salvage yard with a pet monster. But the rumor keeps investigators looking the wrong way, and the Gaspars are too nice to correct anyone.

**Adventure hooks:**
- The party is hired to *prove* Gaspar Reclamations is a Donjon front. Which will fail — because it isn't.
- Don Jon meets Don Leto (see [[Don Leto di Frommagio]]). Nobody expected the rats.
- The Gaspars find out. They are not happy. Their pet is *very* not happy.
