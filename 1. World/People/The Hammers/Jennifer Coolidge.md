**Description:**
The HOA president of [[The Hammers]]. Wants the Boroughs to look nice. Cares deeply about drapes, hedges, and the *tone* of a neighborhood meeting. Has no idea how to run a Robin-Hood organization.

**Her blind spot:**
Her own assistant, [[Kay Aren]], is quietly plotting to level the Boroughs and put up an apartment complex. Jennifer thinks Kay is *such a hard worker*.

**Adventure hooks:**
- She hires the party for something aesthetic that turns out to be tactically important.
- She has to give a speech that will make or break the Hammers' next action. She's not ready.
- Kay's plan surfaces at an HOA meeting. Jennifer has to choose sides in real time.
