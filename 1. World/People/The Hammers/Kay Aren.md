**Description:**
Jennifer's assistant. Runs the operational side of the HOA because Jennifer can't. Universally trusted. Universally underestimated.

**Her actual plan:**
Destroy the Boroughs from the inside — mismanage the HOA into collapse, wait for the neighborhood to be condemned, and put up a high-rise development. She's patient. She's playing a decade-long game.

**Adventure hooks:**
- The party's contract with the Hammers goes sideways in ways that only make sense if someone at the top is sabotaging.
- Kay tries to hire the party for a job that *looks* Hammers-approved but isn't.
- A PC catches Kay meeting with a developer at [[The Man-o'-War]].
