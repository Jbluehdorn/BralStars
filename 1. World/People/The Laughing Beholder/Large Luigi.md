**Description:**
A beholder who decided that running a tavern was more interesting than world domination. Gregarious, dramatic, genuinely enjoys good company. Absolutely no patience for troublemakers, whom he disintegrates without ceremony.

Knows everyone on the Rock. Never forgets a face, a name, or a debt. Eye stalks always doing something — scanning, winking at regulars, glaring daggers at someone with an unpaid tab.

**The thing under his basement:**
The tunnels below the Laughing Beholder connect to an **ancient illithid warren** (see [[Rock of Bral]] — the Rock was once an illithid colony). At the bottom of that warren sits the **[[Illithid Crucible]]** — the source of the [[The Di Frommagio Crime Family|rat mafia]] beneath his feet.

Does Luigi know? He set the party on it in Session 1 like it was a rat problem, not an alien-artifact problem. Either he genuinely thinks it's rats, or he's using adventurers to solve a much bigger problem without saying so. Both are on-brand.

**Motivations:**
- Run the best damn tavern in Wildspace.
- Maintain [[The Laughing Beholder]] as neutral ground.
- Collect interesting stories and strange drinks from across the spheres.

**Rumors:**
- Luigi was exiled from his beholder colony for "excessive friendliness."
- He keeps a magical ledger of every debt ever owed to him — and cheats on his taxes in it (see [[Session 1 - Rats in the Cellar]]).
- His disintegration ray has a "warning setting." Most people only get to test that once.
