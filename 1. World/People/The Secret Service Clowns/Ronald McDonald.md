**Description:**
The head clown. Runs [[Prince Andru]]'s secret police. Takes vengeance on others for his own pure-white appearance — a lifelong resentment he cannot articulate and refuses to examine.

**The unnerving fact:**
He is immortal. He reproduces asexually. There is more than one Ronald walking the Rock at any given time and even he cannot tell you exactly how many.

**Adventure hooks:**
- [[Fairy Floss]] wants him dead. Which one?
- Two Ronalds are seen in different districts on the same night — which is real?
- Something is *making* Ronalds. Or he's making himself. There is a *lot* of him.
