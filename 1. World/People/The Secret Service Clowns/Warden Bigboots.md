**Description:**
Warden of the barracks at [[Starhaven]]. Got the job because of his family. Has size-2 shoes and a voice that squeaks when he's excited (which is most of the time). Deeply, unmistakably unhinged.

**Current obsession:**
He wants to turn [[Ringmaster Blorg]] into a pair of custom crocodile-skin boots. He is *actively planning* this. Blorg does not know.

**Adventure hooks:**
- The party overhears his plan for Blorg. Do they warn the crocodile?
- Bigboots requests the party's help with "a project."
- His unhinged energy makes him a great inside-Starhaven contact — right up until it makes him a liability.
