> ##### Captain Barnacle
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | Race | Human |
> | Occupation | "Retired" pirate captain (allegedly) |

**Description:**
A beloved fixture at Low City bars — spins tall tales of piracy, storms, and vendettas, and *very* clearly still has the moves if pushed. People buy him drinks.

**The catch:**
He is a complete fraud. He's a **High City elite** slumming it for entertainment. Never a pirate. Never at sea. Genuinely dangerous anyway.

**Tells:**
- He doesn't drink. Ever. Waters down a rum for effect and never touches it.
- He tips *extravagantly.* Nobody who's actually seen him tip believes he was ever a broke sailor.

**Adventure hooks:**
- A PC calls his bluff. Now what?
- He recognizes someone from his real life at a Low City bar and panics.
- He hires the party for a "job" that is just a story he wants to be able to tell.
