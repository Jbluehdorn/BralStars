> ##### Jawless Jerry
> | | |
> |---|---|
> | Race | Coyote (wild-shape stuck) |
> | Real identity | A druid with a cleft palate |
> | Status | Local cryptid, Low City |
> | Reputation | Great fortune or great disaster follows a sighting |

**Description:**
A coyote with no jaw. Roams Low City after dark. Superstitious dockworkers swear that if you see her, the next day brings either a windfall or a catastrophe. Never a middle outcome.

**The truth:**
She's a druid with a cleft palate whose wild-shape misfired — she couldn't fully complete the transformation, and got stuck. She isn't bad at magic. She's *hurt by* magic. The jaw isn't missing; it never formed correctly on the way over.

**What she's actually doing:**
Stealing **catalytic converters** from Wildspace ships. Her ones magically convert the ship into a Cadillac. Nobody knows why. She may not know why. The rate of Cadillac-conversion incidents on the Rock has become statistically noticeable.

**Adventure hooks:**
- A PC's ship becomes a Cadillac. Who do you even complain to?
- Someone hires the party to bag the cryptid.
- Jerry can be helped. Anyone who solves her transformation gets a druid ally who owes them a life.
