**Description:**
The face of the [[Mages' Hall]]. Publicly one of many wizards. Privately: *the* wizard. Every other "mage" at the Hall is one of his projections — illusions maintained to keep the tuition and the political cover flowing.

**In private:**
He's a recluse. Walks around naked. Talks to no one. Runs the entire Mages' Hall as a one-man theatrical production between projections.

**The problem:**
He's been **embezzling funds** from the Rock's elites for years. [[The Hammers]] figured it out and now quietly **skim off the top** of every embezzled coin. He knows they know. He does nothing about it — the exposure would ruin him.

**Adventure hooks:**
- A student at the Mages' Hall notices two "different" professors have the exact same speech quirk.
- The Hammers need the party to protect DC — because a dead DC means no more skim.
- A rival wants to expose DC. Do the party defend the fraud or burn it down?
