# Overview
An improbable, beautiful lake set inside the Rock — freshwater, calm surface, faintly luminescent at night. Nobody knows where the water comes from. Nobody knows how deep it is. Nobody has surveyed past the third buoy and returned to describe it.

# Who's here
- [[The Lovers of the Lake]] — a small cult that worships the water.
- Fishing families who *do not* go past the second buoy.
- Occasional pilgrims who've heard the Lake grants requests.

# Adventure hooks
- Someone needs an item retrieved from past the third buoy.
- The Lake requests a favor — see [[The Lovers of the Lake]].
- Something in the Lake responds to [[Illithid Crucible|the humming beneath the Rock]].
