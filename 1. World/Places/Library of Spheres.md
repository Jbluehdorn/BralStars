# Overview
The Rock's definitive collection of star charts, spelljamming routes, and crystal-sphere maps. Every captain worth their tack cites the Library at some point. Every captain worth their tack has also been thrown out for arguing with a librarian.

# What lives here
- **Every mapped route** in Realmspace and a dozen adjacent spheres.
- Historical charts of the Rock itself — including a few that hint at the [[Rock of Bral|illithid layout beneath]].
- A "closed stacks" section requiring a Librarian's blessing.

# Adventure hooks
- A route the party needs was *quietly removed* from public copies.
- The Library's oldest chart of the Rock's interior shows chambers nobody has ever visited.
- Someone is defacing charts to send other captains into ambushes.
