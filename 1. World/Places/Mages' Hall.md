# Overview
The Rock's official school of magic. Impressive halls, well-appointed lecture rooms, a robust tuition schedule, and an alarming number of visiting scholars who all speak with the *exact same cadence.*

**The secret:** [[Professor DC]] is the only real mage. Every other faculty member is one of his projections. He runs the whole institution as a one-man show and embezzles the surplus.

**Who else knows:** [[The Hammers]], who quietly skim off the embezzlement.

# Adventure hooks
- A student notices something. Do they get to graduate?
- A "faculty member" is asked to appear in two places at once.
- Someone attacks the Hall — and only *one* wizard fights back.
