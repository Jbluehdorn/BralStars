# Overview
The **Rock of Bral** is a mile-long asteroid drifting through the crystal sphere of Realmspace. Trading hub, port, black market, home to merchants, pirates, nobles, adventurers, and refugees from a hundred worlds.

Three districts stacked bottom-to-top of the asteroid. And a **hollow, humming interior** nobody in polite company talks about.

## The Economy
- **You can barter with anything, and everything is measured by weight.** Coin works. Gems work. A bag of nails works, if the nails weigh right. Every stall on the Rock has a scale.
- Prince Andru's officials formalized the system decades ago to keep trade moving without a stable currency across so many spheres.

## The Hollow Center
- Old mining operations carved out most of the interior. See [[The Tunnels]].
- Some chambers down there **predate the mining** — cyclopean architecture, dead alien machinery, corridors that hum.
- **The Rock was once an illithid colony.** Nobody knows when. Nobody remembers. Nobody in charge is eager to remember. See [[Illithid Crucible]] for one very much surviving piece.

## The Failing Gravity
- The Rock's magical gravity generator is **failing.**
- People have started **falling off** — mostly at the edges, mostly at night. Officially: freak accidents.
- **Rumor:** the fallen are all from the **naval base** — the same people responsible for maintaining the fields. Whoever's dropping isn't a random citizen. It's the crew.
- Nobody has connected this publicly to the [[Xenotermination Ltd.|mining]] undermining the anchor. Yet.

# History
Started as a pirate base. Grew into a semi-legitimate city-state under the Bral royal family. Was, long before that, an **illithid colony** — a fact that nobody remembers and everybody's descendants live on top of.
