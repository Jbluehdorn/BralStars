# Overview
The **Rock of Bral** is a mile-long asteroid that serves as a major trading hub drifting through the crystal sphere of Realmspace. Equal parts city, port, and black market, the Rock is home to merchants, pirates, nobles, adventurers, and refugees from a hundred different worlds.

The Rock is divided into three districts stacked from bottom to top of the asteroid:

## Low City
The docks, warehouses, and rough taverns. Home to dockworkers, sailors, and criminals. The **Docks** are always busy with spelljammer ships arriving and departing.

Notable locations:
- [[The Laughing Beholder]] — the most famous tavern on the Rock; also the adventurers' posting board
- [[The Docks]] — the main port for spelljamming vessels
- [[The Rat's Maze]] — a labyrinthine slum district

## Middle City
The commercial heart of the Rock. Merchants, craftspeople, inns, and temples fill these streets.

Notable locations:
- [[The Grand Bazaar]] — sprawling open-air market
- [[Temple Row]] — a strip of temples to various gods
- [[The Gambler's Den]] — high-stakes gambling hall

## High City
The seat of power. Home to nobles, the royal palace, and the finest estates.

Notable locations:
- [[The Palace of Bral]] — home of Prince Andru
- [[The Noble Quarter]] — estates of the powerful merchant families
- [[The Arena]] — gladiatorial combat for public entertainment

# History
The Rock of Bral began as a pirate base and gradually grew into a legitimate (or semi-legitimate) city-state under the rule of the Bral royal family. Prince Andru currently rules with a careful balance of power between the noble houses, the guilds, and the military.
