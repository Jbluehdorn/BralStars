# Overview
Started as a legitimate theater. Turns out costumes were the profitable half. Now it's a costume shop that services the entire Rock — festivals, galas, disguises, and, if you know who to ask, *very good* disguises.

# What you can get here
- A costume for any occasion, any species, any budget.
- A *forgery-quality* uniform or livery — if the shopkeeper likes you.
- Rumors, in trade for the right kind of costume order.

# Adventure hooks
- The party needs to infiltrate somewhere. This is the first stop.
- A costume ordered by someone else looks *exactly* like a PC's face.
- The shopkeeper is being blackmailed for the client list.
