# Overview
**Starhaven** is the sprawling estate at the highest point of the Rock — palace, grounds, barracks, and the infamous **hedge maze.** It is [[Prince Andru]]'s home and the operating base of [[The Secret Service Clowns]].

Officially: the Palace of Bral. Officially: a place of state functions, ballrooms, and treaties.

Unofficially: [[Ronald McDonald]] and his clowns take dissidents into the hedge maze and *chase them* through it. People do not come back out of the hedge maze.

# Key Areas
- **The Palace proper** — ballrooms, council chamber, treasury, private quarters (see [[The Palace of Bral]] for the diplomatic surface).
- **The Barracks** — run by [[Warden Bigboots]], size-2s and all.
- **The Hedge Maze** — the disappearing ground.
- **The Prince's private garden** — Kyra's canon: **something very secret** at the center. Nobody at Session 0 knew what. Andru does.

# Adventure hooks
- A friend of a PC goes into the maze and doesn't come out.
- A summons arrives — the Prince wants "a friendly walk in the garden."
- A Groundhog gestures the party toward a tunnel that surfaces *inside* the estate.
- What is at the center of the maze?
