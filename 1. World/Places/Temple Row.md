# Overview
**Temple Row** is the spiritual heart of the Rock of Bral — a long street lined with shrines, temples, and chapels dedicated to gods from scores of different worlds. The air is perpetually thick with incense, chimes, and the competing sounds of prayers and chanting in a dozen languages.

One might find a dwarven hall dedicated to Moradin, a celestial shrine to Celestian (god of wanderers and Wildspace), an elven glade beneath enchanted glass for Corellon, modest shrines to Fharlanghn, Tyr, Avandra, and stranger altars to powers with no common names. The coexistence is uneasy in places — theological disputes occasionally flare into open conflict.

Temples on the Row offer healing, blessings, divination, and ritual services — for a donation. They are also deeply entangled in the Rock's politics, each faith working to extend its influence over the city's residents.

# Key Temples
- **The Shrine of Celestian** — the most prominent temple, patron of spelljammers and wanderers; always busy with sailors seeking safe passage
- **The Hall of Moradin** — dwarven temple; also serves as a community center for the Rock's substantial dwarf population
- **The House of Many Faces** — a temple to no single deity; provides services for faiths too small to maintain their own building

# Adventure Hooks
- A relic stolen from one temple has turned up in another — accusations are flying
- A new cult has established a foothold on Temple Row; their god is not in any known pantheon
- A priest has information the party needs, but will only share it in exchange for a favor
