# Overview
The **Arena** is a massive circular structure that draws crowds from every district of the Rock. Gladiators — volunteers, convicted criminals, and contracted fighters — face off against each other or against monsters imported from across Wildspace. The roar of the crowd during a major event is audible from blocks away.

The public stands are open to all who can pay the entrance fee; private boxes ring the upper level for nobles and wealthy patrons who prefer to watch in comfort. Below the Arena floor, a network of passages holds holding cells, preparation rooms, and the mechanisms used to introduce monsters or change the terrain for special events.

The Arena's owners have a constant appetite for new talent and dangerous creatures, and they're not shy about approaching skilled fighters or adventurers who've made a name for themselves.

# Notable Events
- **The Weekly Gauntlet** — a team event where groups of fighters face escalating waves of opponents
- **The Grand Exhibition** — monthly; features exotic monsters and high-profile bouts
- **The Undercards** — unofficial bouts held in the passages below, heavily bet upon

# Adventure Hooks
- A fighter the party knows has been contracted and can't get out of their Arena obligations
- A monster scheduled for the next Exhibition escaped its holding cell
- Someone is fixing fights — and the wrong people are starting to lose
