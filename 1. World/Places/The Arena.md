# Overview
A massive circular structure. Draws crowds from every district. Public stands for the paying crowd, private boxes ringing the upper level for nobles who prefer their bloodsport with a wine list.

**The open secret:**
The fights are **scripted, WWE-style.** Fixed outcomes, choreographed spectacle. [[The Cirque]] runs the whole operation and takes a cut of the gambling on both sides. The crowd doesn't know — or pretends not to. Money on the fights is *very* real; consequences for figuring it out are worse.

# Notable Events
- **The Weekly Gauntlet** — team event, escalating waves. Big draw, big fixes.
- **The Grand Exhibition** — monthly. Exotic monsters, high-profile bouts, biggest scripts.
- **The Undercards** — unofficial bouts in the passages below. Genuinely unscripted. Genuinely bloody.

# Adventure Hooks
- A fighter the party knows has been contracted and can't get out of their Arena obligations.
- A monster scheduled for the next Exhibition escaped its holding cell.
- Someone is *un-fixing* fights — losing when they were supposed to win, or vice versa. The wrong people are losing money.
- A PC discovers the script. What's it worth to the Cirque to keep them quiet?
