# Overview
A tight-knit residential district that grew up around the Rock's working population. The Boroughs are where the docks' dockworkers, the Bazaar's small merchants, and the Mages' Hall's junior staff *actually live*. Home base of [[The Hammers]].

**Tensions:**
- The [[The Hammers|Hammers']] HOA runs the block. Meetings run long.
- [[Kay Aren]] is quietly engineering the Boroughs' collapse to redevelop the land.
- High City elites eye the Boroughs as prime future real estate.

# Adventure hooks
- An eviction notice appears on a block that shouldn't be for sale.
- The Hammers ask the party to defend a Borough house from "developers" (i.e. thugs).
- Someone in the Boroughs remembers what the land was *before* the neighborhood.
