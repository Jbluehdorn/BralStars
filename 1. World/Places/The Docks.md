# Overview
The **Docks** are the beating heart of the Rock of Bral's economy — massive wooden platforms bolted to the exposed stone of the asteroid, ringed with magical anchoring wards that hold spelljamming vessels of every size. Ships arrive and depart around the clock: merchant galleons, pirate cutters, military frigates, and strange alien craft from distant crystal spheres.

The noise never stops. Stevedores shout, tools clang against hulls, pack animals bray under heavy loads, and rival crews come to blows in the alleys between warehouses. Customs officials collect docking fees and inspect cargo — though a modest bribe can significantly simplify that process.

# Key Areas
- **The Repair Yards** — drydocks and workshops where damaged ships are patched up; the best artificers and shipwrights on the Rock work here
- **The Customs House** — where ships register, pay fees, and declare cargo
- **The Cargo Warehouses** — massive stone buildings lining the dock face; some are legitimate, others are not
