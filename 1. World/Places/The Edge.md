# Overview
A brothel. It caters to a *very* specific kink. The staff will not name it in mixed company and neither will this note.

The Edge is remarkable less for what happens inside than for who quietly walks in and out — half the Rock's power players have a standing appointment. Blackmail material walks in here nightly. None of it ever gets used, because the Edge's discretion has, so far, been perfect.

# Adventure hooks
- Someone finally breaks the Edge's discretion. Whose life ends first?
- A PC follows a mark inside. Now they know something the mark can't afford them to know.
- The Edge itself needs a favor from the party — the kind of favor only outsiders can do.
