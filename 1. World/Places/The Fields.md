# Overview
The Rock has to put its dead somewhere. It puts them here.

The soil at the Fields is the most fertile on the asteroid, and the Rock's small agricultural plots depend on the runoff. Nobody talks about the sourcing.

# Who works the Fields
- Undertakers, gravediggers, a resident cleric.
- Compost merchants who avoid eye contact with the undertakers.
- Rumor: [[The Groundhogs]] use the Fields as a surface access point.

# Adventure hooks
- A body was buried that shouldn't have been (or wasn't buried that should have been).
- Someone the party knew "died" — but the plot is empty.
- The compost is producing something *unexpected* this season.
