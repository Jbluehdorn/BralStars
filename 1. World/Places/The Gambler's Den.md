# Overview
The **Gambler's Den** is a two-story establishment on one of Middle City's busier avenues, recognizable by its flickering magical lanterns and heavy velvet curtains that block any view from the street. Silver bells chime whenever the door opens. The main floor is packed with dice tables, card games, and spinning Astro-Wheel contraptions; a private gallery upstairs hosts high-stakes games for those who can afford the buy-in.

The Den draws a broad clientele: merchants, off-duty mercenaries, bored nobles, spelljammer crews blowing their last haul, and sharp-eyed professionals looking for marks. Security is visible and well-armed, with additional magical protections running throughout the building.

The proprietor, **Mila "Quickfingers" Xelbrin**, has a reputation for running honest odds — which is precisely why people keep coming back. The side rooms are a different story.

# Notable NPCs
- **Mila "Quickfingers" Xelbrin** — half-elf owner; charming, sharp, not above a rigged game when it suits her
- **Brot** — giff pit boss; enormous, good-humored, terrifying when provoked

# Adventure Hooks
- A noble house is secretly rigging games to launder money or ruin a rival
- A valuable item was put up as collateral on a debt and lost at the tables
- A criminal faction is muscling in on the Den's profits; Mila needs quiet help
