# Overview
The **Grand Bazaar** is one of the largest open marketplaces in all of Wildspace — a riot of color, noise, and competing smells. Hundreds of stalls and permanent shop fronts cover every surface of this sprawling Middle City district, selling goods from dozens of crystal spheres.

You can find nearly anything here: spelljamming components, magical trinkets, exotic foods, rare textiles, strange live animals, ancient maps, and oddities that defy classification. Haggling is not just expected — offering the asking price is considered vaguely insulting.

Pickpockets operate constantly throughout the Bazaar, and the local constabulary maintains a visible but largely performative presence. Truly illegal goods — slaves, banned magical items, stolen property — change hands through subtler channels, often via brokers who operate from seemingly ordinary stalls.

# What's For Sale
- Spelljamming components and repair supplies
- Adventuring gear and weapons (mundane and magical)
- Maps of charted and uncharted regions
- Exotic goods from distant crystal spheres
- Information and rumors (ask the right vendors)
- Passage on ships (unofficial broker stalls near the east end)
