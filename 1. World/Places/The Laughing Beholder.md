# Overview
The **Laughing Beholder** is the most famous establishment on the Rock of Bral — a massive tavern and inn whose proprietor is a beholder named **Large Luigi**. Despite the obvious danger of an eye tyrant running a bar, Luigi is surprisingly jovial and has built the Laughing Beholder into a true institution.

The Laughing Beholder serves as the **central hub for the adventuring community** on the Rock. Its notorious **Adventurers' Board** is covered in job postings, bounties, expedition proposals, and rumors from across Wildspace.

# The Adventurers' Board
The cork board near the entrance is where:
- Merchants post contracts for escort or retrieval jobs
- Nobles post bounties on wanted individuals
- Explorers share rumors of discovered dungeons, ruins, or resources
- Adventurers post looking-for-party notices

# Notable NPCs
- **Large Luigi** — the beholder proprietor; surprisingly friendly, absolutely will disintegrate troublemakers
- **Marta** — the head bartender; knows everyone and everything
