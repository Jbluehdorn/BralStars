# Overview
Named for the spelljamming ship class it definitely could not fit inside, the Man-o'-War is the restaurant where deals get made, marriages get arranged, and quiet reputations get destroyed over the amuse-bouche. The food is excellent. The napkins are stiff enough to stand up on their own. The bill is not itemized.

# Who you'll see here
- Elites of every faction — [[The Donjon Crime Family|Donjons]] and Noble Quarter families both dine here without acknowledging one another.
- [[Kay Aren]] has been spotted taking meetings that don't fit her HOA cover.
- [[Captain Barnacle]] absolutely does not come here. Ever. (Because his real face would be recognized.)

# Adventure hooks
- The party is hired to eavesdrop on a specific table.
- Someone gets poisoned. Everyone in the room had motive.
- A reservation was made in a PC's name. They didn't make it.
