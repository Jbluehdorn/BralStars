# Overview
The **Noble Quarter** is High City's most exclusive district — a neighborhood of grand mansions, private gardens, and members-only clubs. The architecture is opulent and varied, reflecting the tastes and origins of residents who have come from dozens of different worlds.

The streets are quiet and well-maintained, patrolled by private guards whose job is to keep out anyone who doesn't belong. Life here is not the raw commerce of the Middle City or the survival calculus of the Low City — it is the slower, more dangerous game of high-society intrigue. Alliances are forged and broken, fortunes change hands in private card rooms, and old grudges are settled through reputation rather than blades (usually).

The major noble houses of the Rock maintain their primary estates here.

# Character of the Quarter
- Private parties and exclusive dinners are the primary social venue
- Reputation matters enormously; a public slight can ruin a house's standing for a generation
- The guards are professional and loyal, but not incorruptible
- Hidden passages and secret rooms are not uncommon in the older estates

# Adventure Hooks
- A party member receives an invitation to a noble's private dinner — likely not a social call
- Something embarrassing (or dangerous) needs to be recovered from inside a noble estate
- Two houses are at the edge of open conflict; someone needs to broker a truce, or light the fuse
