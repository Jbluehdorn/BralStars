# Overview
The **Palace of Bral** sits at the highest point of the asteroid, its towers and domes clad in gold, marble, and pale crystal visible from almost anywhere on the Rock. Lush enclosed gardens and intricate mosaics adorn the grounds — a deliberate display of wealth and permanence in the otherwise transient world of Wildspace.

The Palace is the seat of government: where laws are made, trade deals are struck, and the political maneuvering of the noble houses plays out under Prince Andru's watchful eye. Ballrooms, council chambers, a treasury, and the Prince's private quarters are all within its walls, surrounded by a substantial and professional palace guard.

Entry requires an invitation, official business, or high enough status to be waved through. The palace staff is discrete and well-compensated for their loyalty.

# Key Areas
- **The Council Chamber** — where [[Prince Andru]] meets with noble representatives and guild heads
- **The Grand Ballroom** — used for state functions and high-society events
- **The War Room** — where the Rock's military affairs are coordinated
- **The Dungeons** — below the palace; rarely spoken of publicly

# Adventure Hooks
- The party receives a summons to the Palace — Prince Andru has a job that requires deniability
- Something has been stolen from the palace treasury; the theft hasn't been made public
- A noble is trying to arrange an "audience" that isn't on the official schedule
