# Overview
The **Palace of Bral** — known to everyone who lives on the Rock simply as **[[Starhaven]]** — sits at the highest point of the asteroid. Gold-clad towers, marble halls, pale crystal domes, walled gardens, and the infamous hedge maze.

This page is the formal / diplomatic surface. For the *operational* estate — the barracks, the hedge maze, [[The Secret Service Clowns]], and where dissidents disappear — see [[Starhaven]].

# Key Areas
- **The Council Chamber** — where [[Prince Andru]] meets noble representatives and guild heads.
- **The Grand Ballroom** — state functions, treaties, weddings-of-convenience.
- **The War Room** — military affairs.
- **The Treasury** — heavily guarded, oddly modest for the reputation.
- **The Dungeons** — rarely spoken of publicly. Also see: the hedge maze, which people don't come out of.

# Adventure Hooks
- The party receives a summons — Andru has a job that requires deniability.
- Something has been stolen from the treasury; the theft hasn't been made public.
- A noble is trying to arrange an audience that isn't on the official schedule.
- Someone the party knows was seen being escorted into the gardens. They haven't been seen since.
