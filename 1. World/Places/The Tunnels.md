# Overview
The Rock is **hollow.** Old mining operations carved out the interior, but the digging stopped when workers started finding things that shouldn't exist — architecture that predates any known settlement, chambers full of dead illithid machinery, corridors that hum.

Today the Tunnels are:
- **Smuggling routes** — ships get hidden at the edges where the tunnels meet Wildspace.
- **Shortcuts** — if you can find a Groundhog, you can cross districts without touching the surface.
- **A refuge** — [[Valkan's Legion]] lives down here.
- **A slow catastrophe** — the mining has undermined the gravity generator (see [[Rock of Bral]]).

# Who walks the Tunnels
- [[The Groundhogs]] — silent guides.
- [[The Di Frommagio Crime Family]] — their hideout is a small part of a *much* larger warren.
- [[Manny]] & [[Ramirez]] — imprinted cockroaches of the drainage channels.
- [[Xenotermination Ltd.]] — mining crews going where they shouldn't.

# Adventure hooks
- A ship the party needs to find is hidden at an edge tunnel — but which one?
- A Groundhog abandons the party in the wrong chamber.
- Something old and illithid-shaped wakes up.
