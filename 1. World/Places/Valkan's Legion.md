# Overview
A hidden settlement deep in [[The Tunnels]]. Home to fugitives, deserters, discarded projects, and anyone else who needed to stop existing on the Rock's official rolls.

**Only [[The Groundhogs]] know the way.** People who arrive by any other route arrived by accident — usually because a Groundhog decided to leave them somewhere, and they happened to walk in the right direction.

# Who lives here
- Deserters (probably including [[Steve]] soon).
- Ex-Cirque members who wouldn't play along with the scripted fights.
- Red Masks who escaped a Clown recruitment.
- One or two people whose names get spoken quietly and never confirmed.

# Adventure hooks
- The party has to *find* Valkan's Legion. Without a Groundhog.
- Someone in the Legion has intel the party desperately needs — and hasn't spoken in years.
- Valkan (whoever they are) sends for the party.
