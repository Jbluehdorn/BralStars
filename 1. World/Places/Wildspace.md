# Overview
**Wildspace** is the vast emptiness between worlds within a crystal sphere. It is airless and gravity-less, traversable only by vessels equipped with **spelljamming helms** — magical devices that allow ships to sail the void.

Wildspace is home to:
- Asteroid fields and small rocks (potential dungeons or settlements)
- Space monsters: neogi, illithid nautiloids, beholders, giant space hamsters
- Pirate fleets and legitimate traders
- Strange phenomena and ancient ruins

# Key Locations in Realmspace Wildspace
- [[Rock of Bral]] — the major port and trade hub
- [[Toril]] — the planet Faerûn sits on
- [[Selûne's Tears]] — trailing cluster of asteroids behind Toril's moon
- [[The Flow]] — the Phlogiston that separates crystal spheres

# Travel Times
Spelljamming travel in Wildspace is measured in days. Inter-sphere travel through the Flow takes weeks to months depending on destination.
