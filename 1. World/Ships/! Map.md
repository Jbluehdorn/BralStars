# Ships of Wildspace

A reference guide to spelljamming vessels you may encounter — or captain — in Wildspace. Ships are grouped by role.

---

## Warships
- [[Hammership]] — the classic pirate/military workhorse
- [[Nautiloid]] — mind flayer vessel; feared across Wildspace
- [[Neogi Deathspider]] — neogi slaver warship
- [[Man-O-War]] — elven imperial warship; fast and deadly

## Explorers & Scouts
- [[Squid Ship]] — nimble and versatile; a common adventurer's vessel
- [[Dragonfly Ship]] — small and fast; popular scout craft
- [[Mosquito Ship]] — tiny one-man vessel
- [[Wasp Ship]] — quick interceptor

## Merchant & Utility
- [[Space Galleon]] — the classic cargo hauler of Wildspace
- [[Tradesman]] — reliable merchant vessel; built for cargo
- [[Bombard]] — Giff siege ship; bristling with heavy weapons

## Exotic & Rare
- [[Swan Ship]] — elegant elven vessel; rarely seen outside elven space
- [[Living Ship]] — grown from magical plants; self-repairing
- [[Tyrant Ship]] — beholder vessel; only a beholder can pilot it
- [[Star Moth]] — sleek elven craft
- [[Turtle Ship]] — heavily armored mobile fortress

## Common Weapons
Most ships are armed with some combination of:
- **Ballista** — bolt-firing siege weapon; long range
- **Mangonel** — stone-throwing catapult; heavy damage
- **Ram** — prow weapon for close combat boarding
