# Overview
The **Bombard** is a siege vessel designed by the **Giff** — hippo-folk mercenaries with an obsessive love of firearms and explosives. The bombard's signature feature is a massive cannon mounted beneath the hull, capable of devastating damage against other ships or fortifications.

The Giff are the primary operators of bombards, and many hire out as mercenary crews. A bombard-equipped force is a serious threat to any fixed installation.

# Notes
- The cannon is the primary weapon; ballistae are secondary
- Giff crews are professional and disciplined — expensive but reliable
- Bombards are slow and not built for rapid maneuvers
- The cannon requires significant powder stores; fire is a real concern aboard
