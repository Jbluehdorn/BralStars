# Overview
The **Dragonfly Ship** is a small, insectoid vessel prized for its speed and low crew requirements. A single pilot can operate a dragonfly ship in an emergency, making it ideal for couriers, scouts, and anyone who needs to move fast and quietly.

It's lightly armed and has minimal cargo space — the dragonfly is not a fighting ship. What it does exceptionally well is *not being there* when something dangerous shows up.

# Notes
- One of the fastest small vessels available
- Low crew minimum makes it useful for solo or small-party operations
- Not built for extended voyages — air envelope is relatively small
- Popular among smugglers who need to outrun pursuit
