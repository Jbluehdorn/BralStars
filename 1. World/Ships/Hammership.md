# Overview
The **Hammership** is perhaps the most iconic spelljamming vessel in Wildspace — a sturdy, hammer-headed ship favored by pirates, mercenaries, and independent captains alike. Its distinctive forward ram makes it excellent for boarding actions, and its broad hull offers solid cargo capacity alongside its weapons.

Hammerships are a common sight around the [[Rock of Bral]]. If you see one closing fast with no signal flags, start praying.

# Typical Crews
- Pirates and privateers
- Mercenary companies
- Independent adventuring crews

# Notes
- The hammer-shaped prow is reinforced for ramming
- Can hold significant cargo in addition to its weapon crew
