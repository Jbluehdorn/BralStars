# Overview
A **Living Ship** is grown rather than built — a vessel cultivated from magically accelerated plants and shaped over years by druids or elven shipwrights. The result is a fully organic spelljamming ship that can slowly repair its own damage by drawing nutrients from the air envelope.

Living ships feel uncanny to non-druids — the hull breathes slightly, roots occasionally sprout in the hold, and the ship seems to react to extreme danger. Rumors suggest that old living ships develop something close to sentience.

# Notes
- Slowly regenerates HP over time (typically 4–10 HP per day)
- The spelljamming helm is grown directly into the captain's chair
- Requires no ore or timber for repairs — just water, light, and time
- Highly prized; elves rarely sell them to outsiders
