# Overview
The **Man-O-War** is the primary warship of the **Elven Imperial Fleet** — sleek, fast, and deadly. Its elegant curves belie its combat effectiveness; a Man-O-War is faster than almost any human-built vessel and its high armor rating makes it extremely hard to damage.

Elven Man-O-Wars are rarely seen around the [[Rock of Bral]] except during periods of Imperial interest in Realmspace. When a fleet of them appears, something significant is happening.

# Encounters
Man-O-Wars are not typically hostile to neutral parties, but the Elven Imperial Fleet takes its mandate seriously. They may stop and inspect ships they deem suspicious, and they have zero tolerance for slave traders.

# Notes
- Elves prefer living-wood construction when possible
- The Imperial Fleet uses standardized Man-O-Wars but individual ships may vary
- Seeing one alone is unusual — they typically patrol in squadrons
