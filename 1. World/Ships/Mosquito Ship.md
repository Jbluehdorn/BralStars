# Overview
The **Mosquito Ship** is the smallest viable spelljamming vessel — a single-pilot craft barely large enough for one person and minimal supplies. Designed for speed above everything else, the mosquito is the fastest ship in common use and the hardest to hit.

Mosquitoes are used as couriers, scouts, and emergency escape vessels. They appear on the black market occasionally — useful to someone who needs to disappear very quickly.

# Notes
- Fastest vessel available; outpaces nearly everything
- No room for passengers or meaningful cargo
- The air envelope barely extends past the hull — long solo voyages are risky
- Rumors suggest some mosquito ships have been modified with extra fuel tanks for extended range
