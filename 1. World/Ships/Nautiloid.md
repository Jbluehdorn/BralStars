# Overview
The **Nautiloid** is the signature vessel of the **mind flayers (illithids)**. Shaped like a cephalopod shell with trailing tentacles, it is unmistakable — and the sight of one emerging from the darkness of Wildspace is enough to send experienced crews into a panic.

Nautiloids are equipped with a **mind blast cone** projecting from the prow — a psychic weapon that can incapacitate entire crews without destroying the ship or its cargo. Mind flayers prefer to take prisoners.

# Encounters
Nautiloids in the vicinity of the [[Rock of Bral]] are a serious threat. They typically operate alone, targeting isolated vessels. A nautiloid that simply observes and doesn't approach may be scouting.

# Notes
- The tentacles trailing behind are partially functional — used for docking and grappling
- Nautiloids can crash-land in water or on soft terrain
- Mind flayers will sacrifice the ship before surrendering it intact
