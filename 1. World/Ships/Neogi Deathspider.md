# Overview
The **Neogi Deathspider** is the warship of the **neogi** — spider-like slavers who are among the most feared predators in Wildspace. The ship is shaped like a massive spider, with eight articulated legs that serve as landing struts. Its hold is designed to transport slaves efficiently and cruelly.

Deathspiders hunt in pairs or alone, targeting merchant vessels and small colonies. They are heavily armed relative to their crew size because neogi rely on their **umber hulk** thralls for much of the grunt work.

# Encounters
Encountering a deathspider is a serious threat. Neogi take prisoners for the slave trade — they won't destroy a valuable prize if they can capture it instead. This can be used against them.

# Notes
- The eight legs fold against the hull in flight
- Neogi crews are typically supplemented by enslaved umber hulks
- A captured deathspider is worth significant money — if you can get it to port
