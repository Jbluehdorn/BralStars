# Overview
The **Space Galleon** is the spelljamming equivalent of the classic ocean-going galleon — a large, reliable cargo hauler that forms the backbone of interstellar trade. What it lacks in speed and maneuverability it makes up for in cargo capacity and crew space.

Most of the merchant ships docking at the [[Rock of Bral]] are space galleons or tradesman-class vessels. They're the lifeblood of commerce between worlds.

# Notes
- Largest commonly-available merchant vessel
- Requires a substantial crew to operate effectively
- Wide enough hull to carry livestock, wagons, or large cargo
- A fully-loaded space galleon is one of the most valuable prizes in Wildspace
