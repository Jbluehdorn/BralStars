# Overview
The **Squid Ship** is the workhorse of independent spelljammers. Named for the two long tentacle-like extensions from its prow (used for grappling and boarding), the squid ship is nimble, versatile, and affordable — making it the most common vessel owned by adventuring crews, independent traders, and small-time pirates.

A used squid ship can be purchased at the [[The Docks]] for a fraction of the cost of a new warship, and parts are widely available on the [[Rock of Bral]].

# Notes
- The forward tentacles can grapple another vessel as a boarding action
- Good balance of combat ability and cargo space
- Widely considered the best "starter ship" for new spelljammers
- Parts are common enough that repairs are rarely a problem near major ports
