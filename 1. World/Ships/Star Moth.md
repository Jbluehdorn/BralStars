# Overview
The **Star Moth** is a sleek elven vessel used as a fast patrol and escort ship. With its distinctive curved silhouette and impressive speed, the Star Moth is the elven equivalent of a frigate — fast enough to intercept most ships and well-armed enough to win the engagement.

Star Moths are more commonly encountered than Man-O-Wars, used by elven trading companies and smaller fleet detachments.

# Notes
- One of the fastest ships in common use
- High maneuverability — can change heading sharply
- Elves typically use these for courier runs and escort missions
- A Star Moth approaching at speed is not necessarily hostile — elves use them for trade too
