# Overview
The **Swan Ship** is an elven vessel — elegant, graceful, and built for long voyages rather than combat. Its distinctive swan-neck prow and sweeping hull lines are unmistakable. Elves use swan ships primarily as transports and diplomatic vessels rather than warships.

Seeing a swan ship at the Rock of Bral typically signals that elven interests are involved in local affairs — whether that's trade, diplomacy, or something more covert.

# Notes
- Built for comfort and range over combat effectiveness
- Lightly armed relative to its size
- The living-wood hull of most swan ships resists fire slightly
- Rarely seen without an escort of smaller elven craft
