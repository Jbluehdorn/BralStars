# Overview
The **Tradesman** is the most common merchant vessel in Wildspace — a no-frills, boxy cargo hauler that prioritizes cargo space over everything else. It's slow, it's ugly, and it makes money. Traders who need to move goods affordably buy or hire tradesmen.

A tradesman without an escort is considered a soft target by pirates, and many merchant companies hire adventurers to ride along as protection.

# Notes
- Maximized for cargo at the expense of speed and defense
- Most come lightly armed — enough to discourage opportunists, not enough for a real fight
- Common sight at every port in Wildspace; parts and repairs are easy to find
- The crew quarters are notoriously cramped
