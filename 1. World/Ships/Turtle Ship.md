# Overview
The **Turtle Ship** is a slow-moving fortress — a heavily armored vessel that absorbs enormous punishment while delivering sustained weapons fire. Its domed shell-like hull gives it excellent armor coverage at the cost of speed and maneuverability.

Turtle ships are used as flagships, blockade vessels, and mobile fortresses by wealthy military powers. They are difficult to destroy and nearly impossible to board successfully from the front.

# Notes
- Highest AC of any common vessel
- Nearly immune to fire from the front and sides
- Extremely slow — anything fast can simply avoid it
- A turtle ship in port is often used as a floating headquarters or prison
