# Overview
The **Tyrant Ship** is built by and for **beholders**. Its spherical hull mirrors their body shape, and — crucially — it can only be piloted by a beholder using its central eye ray as the "spelljamming helm." No other creature can operate it.

Tyrant ships are rare, but terrifying. A beholder crew uses the ship's weapons alongside their own natural eye rays, making tyrant ships arguably the most dangerous vessel per-crew-member in Wildspace.

# Notes
- Only operable by beholders — useless as a prize unless you have a beholder ally
- The spherical hull is reinforced and provides all-around coverage
- Beholder crews are extremely dangerous in boarding situations
- Tyrant ships are occasionally encountered near the Rock, as beholders maintain interests in the city (see [[Large Luigi]])
