# Overview
The **Wasp Ship** is a small, fast raider — built for hit-and-run attacks rather than sustained combat. Its insectoid profile and high speed make it excellent for intercepting prey, harassing larger vessels, or extracting a crew from a dangerous situation quickly.

Wasp ships are popular with pirates who want speed over firepower, and with small adventuring crews who need to move fast.

# Notes
- Very fast for its class — hard to run from
- Lightly armored; not built to take punishment
- Low crew requirement makes it practical for small groups
- Can land on solid ground as well as water
