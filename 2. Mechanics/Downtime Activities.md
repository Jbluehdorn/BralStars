# Downtime Activities

Between sessions, each character may perform **one downtime activity** per week of in-game time that passes.

## Available Activities

### Carousing
Spend time socializing in the taverns and social spaces of the Rock of Bral.
- **Cost:** 10–100 gp (choose your spending level)
- **Effect:** Gain contacts, hear rumors, improve faction standing
- **Risk:** May attract unwanted attention

### Crafting
Create items using your proficiencies.
- **Cost:** Material costs (half the item's value)
- **Time:** Varies by item complexity
- **Requires:** Appropriate tool proficiency

### Research
Dig into libraries, sages, or contacts to learn about a topic.
- **Cost:** 25 gp per week
- **Effect:** Gain information about a location, creature, item, or faction
- **Requires:** Access to a library or sage

### Training
Practice skills or learn new ones.
- **Cost:** 25 gp per week
- **Time:** Weeks or months depending on what's being learned

### Working
Earn coin doing odd jobs in the city.
- **Cost:** None
- **Effect:** Earn 10–25 gp per week depending on skills

### Crime
Attempt a heist, con, or other illegal job.
- **Cost:** None
- **Effect:** Varies wildly — could be very profitable or very bad

### Religious Service
Serve at a temple on [[Temple Row]].
- **Cost:** None
- **Effect:** Improve standing with a deity's church; possible boons

### Recuperating
Rest and heal injuries, diseases, or other ailments.
- **Cost:** None (just time)
- **Effect:** Recover from lingering injuries or magical afflictions
