# Hex Exploration

When adventurers venture into **uncharted Wildspace**, the region is divided into hexes representing areas of space roughly 12 hours of spelljammer travel across.

## Exploration Procedure
Each hex takes **1 day** to fully explore.

1. **Navigate** to the hex (Survival or Arcana check DC 12 to avoid getting turned around)
2. **Search** the hex — roll on the appropriate encounter table
3. **Chart** the hex — add it to the party's map
4. Share the map with other players after the session

## Hex Contents (Roll d6)
| d6 | Contents |
|----|----------|
| 1–2 | Empty void — nothing here |
| 3 | Asteroid field — possible resources, possible danger |
| 4 | Single asteroid — may have caves, ruins, or creatures |
| 5 | Interesting phenomenon — strange radiation, gravity well, or planar anomaly |
| 6 | Discovery — a significant location (dungeon, settlement, ruin, derelict ship) |

## Revealing a Hex
- A hex is **"known"** once any party has explored it and posted their findings
- All subsequent parties benefit from the prior exploration (no need to re-roll)
- Hexes with discoveries may change if a party interacts with the contents

## Resources Found
Asteroids may contain:
- Ores and minerals (sellable at [[The Grand Bazaar]])
- Crystal formations with magical properties
- Fresh water ice (vital for long journeys)
- Pre-existing installations (ruins, old mining camps, etc.)
