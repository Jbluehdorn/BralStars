# Actions in Combat
On your turn you can **move** up to your speed and take **one action**. You may also take one **bonus action** if a feature grants it, and one **reaction** per round.

## Standard Actions
| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Attack** | Make one or more weapon attacks |
| **Cast a Spell** | Cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action |
| **Dash** | Gain extra movement equal to your speed |
| **Disengage** | Your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of the turn |
| **Dodge** | Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage (if you can see the attacker); you have advantage on Dex saves |
| **Help** | Aid an ally — they gain advantage on their next ability check or attack roll this turn |
| **Hide** | Make a Stealth check to become hidden |
| **Ready** | Prepare an action to trigger on a specified condition (uses your reaction when triggered) |
| **Search** | Devote your attention to finding something (Perception or Investigation check) |
| **Use an Object** | Interact with a second object (first interaction is free) |
| **Improvise** | Anything not listed — DM determines how it resolves |

## Free Interaction
Once per turn, you can interact with **one object for free** (draw a weapon, open a door, pick up an item, etc.)

## Two-Weapon Fighting
When you take the Attack action with a light melee weapon, you can use your **bonus action** to attack with a different light melee weapon in your other hand (no ability modifier to damage unless negative).
