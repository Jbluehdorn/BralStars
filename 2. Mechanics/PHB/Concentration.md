# Concentration
Some spells require **concentration** to maintain their effect. If you lose concentration, the spell ends.

## Losing Concentration
- **Casting another concentration spell** — immediately ends the previous one
- **Taking damage** — make a Constitution saving throw (DC = 10 or half the damage taken, whichever is higher). Failure ends the spell.
- **Being [[Incapacitated]] or killed**

## Concentration Saving Throw
- DC = **10** or **half the damage taken**, whichever is higher
- Add your **Constitution modifier** (and Proficiency if you have War Caster or similar)

## Helpful Feats / Features
- **War Caster** — advantage on concentration saves, can cast spells as opportunity attacks
- **Resilient (Constitution)** — proficiency on Con saves
- **Aura of Protection (Paladin 6+)** — adds Charisma modifier to all saving throws including concentration
