# Cover
Cover provides a bonus to AC and Dexterity saving throws.

| Cover | Bonus | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Half Cover** | +2 AC and Dex saves | Behind a low wall, large furniture, a creature, or a narrow tree trunk |
| **Three-Quarters Cover** | +5 AC and Dex saves | Behind a portcullis, arrow slit, or thick tree trunk |
| **Full Cover** | Can't be targeted directly | Completely hidden behind an obstacle |

## Notes
- A target has cover only if the obstacle is **between** the attacker and the target
- Cover is determined from the **attacker's point of view** — different attackers may see different cover
- Full cover doesn't protect against area-of-effect spells that don't require attack rolls (e.g. *Fireball*)
