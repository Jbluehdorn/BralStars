# Death Saving Throws
When a character drops to **0 hit points**, they fall [[Unconscious]] and must make death saving throws at the start of each of their turns.

## The Roll
- Roll a **d20** (no modifiers apply unless a feature specifically says so)
- **10 or higher** = success
- **9 or lower** = failure
- **Natural 20** = regain 1 hit point and regain consciousness
- **Natural 1** = counts as **two failures**

## Stabilizing
- **3 successes** → the character is **stable** (unconscious but no longer dying)
- **3 failures** → the character **dies**
- Successes and failures don't need to be consecutive, but reset on a long rest

## Being Healed
- Any healing (including being stabilized by another creature using Medicine DC 10) stops death saving throws
- The character regains consciousness if they regain any hit points

## Taking Damage at 0 HP
- Taking damage while at 0 hp counts as a **failure**
- A **critical hit** at 0 hp counts as **two failures**
