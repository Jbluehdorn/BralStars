# Exhaustion
Exhaustion is measured in six levels. Each effect is **cumulative**.

| Level | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Disadvantage on ability checks |
| 2 | Speed halved |
| 3 | Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws |
| 4 | Hit point maximum halved |
| 5 | Speed reduced to 0 |
| 6 | Death |

**Removing Exhaustion:** Finishing a long rest removes one level of exhaustion, provided the creature has food and water.
