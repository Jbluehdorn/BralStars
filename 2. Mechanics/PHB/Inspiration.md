# Inspiration
Inspiration is a reward the DM can grant to a player for **exceptional roleplay, clever thinking, or leaning into their character's traits/flaws/bonds/ideals**.

## Using Inspiration
- You either **have** inspiration or you **don't** — it doesn't stack
- Spend it to gain **advantage on any attack roll, saving throw, or ability check**
- Must be declared **before** the roll

## Giving Inspiration Away
- A character with inspiration can give it to another player as a **free action** at any time
- Good for rewarding teamwork or another player's great moment

## Earning Inspiration
The DM awards inspiration when a player:
- Acts in accordance with their personality traits, ideals, bonds, or flaws (even at a cost)
- Does something genuinely creative or unexpected that makes the table better
- Accepts a complication or hardship for the sake of character consistency
