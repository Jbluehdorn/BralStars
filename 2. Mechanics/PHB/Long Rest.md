# Long Rest
A long rest is a period of extended downtime lasting **at least 8 hours**, during which a character sleeps for at least 6 hours and performs no more than 2 hours of light activity.

## What You Recover
- All lost **hit points**
- All spent **Hit Dice** up to half your total (minimum 1)
- All expended **spell slots**
- All class features that recharge on a long rest

## Restrictions
- A character can only benefit from **one long rest per 24 hours**
- Must have at least 1 hit point to begin a long rest
- Interruptions of 1+ hour of strenuous activity (combat, spells, travel) require starting over

## DM Notes
- Long rests in dangerous territory may require watches — interrupt on a failed Perception check
- In the West Marches format, a long rest typically happens back on the [[Rock of Bral]] between sessions
