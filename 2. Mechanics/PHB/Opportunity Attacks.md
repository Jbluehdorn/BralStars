# Opportunity Attacks
When a creature you can see **moves out of your reach**, you may use your **reaction** to make one melee attack against it.

## Triggering an Opportunity Attack
- Creature moves out of your reach voluntarily
- Does **not** trigger if the creature teleports or is moved by an outside force (e.g. shoved, dragged, or *Thunderwave*)

## Avoiding Opportunity Attacks
- Take the **Disengage** action — your movement won't provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of the turn
- Some features (Cunning Action, Step of the Wind) allow Disengage as a bonus action

## Notes
- You can only make one opportunity attack per trigger (one reaction per round)
- Opportunity attacks happen **before** the triggering movement is completed
- Reach weapons (e.g. polearms) trigger at 10 ft. reach, not 5 ft.
