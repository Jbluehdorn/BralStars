# Short Rest
A short rest is a period of downtime lasting **at least 1 hour**, during which a character does nothing more strenuous than eating, drinking, reading, or tending to wounds.

## What You Can Do
- Spend one or more **Hit Dice** (up to your maximum)
- For each Hit Die spent, roll it and add your **Constitution modifier** — recover that many hit points
- Some class features (e.g. Warlock spell slots, Fighter's Second Wind via Action Surge) recharge on a short rest

## What You Cannot Do
- Take a short rest if you've taken one in the last hour (you need 1 hour of light activity)

## DM Notes
- Track time carefully — short rests in dungeons can be interrupted
- Consider whether the location is safe enough to allow one
