# Pointcrawl Navigation
A **pointcrawl** is an exploration structure where the world is represented as a network of **named locations connected by routes**, rather than a full hex grid. It's useful for navigating the Rock of Bral itself or known regions of Wildspace.

## How It Works
- Locations are **nodes** — places of interest (districts, asteroids, derelict ships, etc.)
- Nodes are connected by **routes** — each route has a travel time and potential hazards
- Players choose which route to take; the DM rolls for encounters along the way

## Travel Along a Route
1. Players declare their destination
2. DM states the approximate travel time
3. Roll for **random encounters** (see [[Random Encounters]]) — once per segment
4. Players arrive and explore the node

## Wildspace Pointcrawl
For known Wildspace routes between the Rock and charted asteroids, use the same system with travel times in hours or days instead of minutes.
