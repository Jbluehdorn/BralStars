# Random Encounters

## Rock of Bral (Low City)

| d10 | Encounter |
|-----|-----------|
| 1 | A pickpocket attempts to lift something from the party |
| 2 | A Juggler's Guild enforcer wants a word |
| 3 | A desperate sailor offers information in exchange for coin |
| 4 | A street vendor is selling suspiciously magical goods |
| 5 | A brawl spills out of a tavern |
| 6 | A Seeker posts a new urgent job on the Adventurers' Board |
| 7 | A noble's guard bumps into a PC and demands an apology |
| 8 | A strange ship docks and unusual passengers disembark |
| 9 | Someone recognizes a PC from a past adventure (reputation check) |
| 10 | A small child offers a "treasure map" for 5 copper |
