# Spelljamming Helm
A **spelljamming helm** is a magical throne-like seat that, when a spellcaster attunes to it, allows them to mentally control a spelljamming vessel and propel it through Wildspace.

## Requirements
- Any creature that can cast at least one spell can attune to a helm
- Attuning takes 1 hour of concentration while seated
- Only **one creature** can be attuned at a time

## What the Pilot Can Do
- Control the ship's speed and direction mentally (no hands required, but eyes must be open)
- Sense the ship's condition as if it were an extension of their own body
- Communicate with the crew through the ship's speaking tubes (if installed)

## Limitations
- While attuned and actively piloting, the creature **cannot cast spells or use magical class features**
- The pilot is aware of threats to the ship but cannot use their own senses beyond the ship's hull
- If the pilot is killed or removed from the helm, the ship coasts forward until someone else attunes
