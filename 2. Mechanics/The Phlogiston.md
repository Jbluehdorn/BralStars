# The Phlogiston
The **Phlogiston** (also called "the Flow") is the multicolored, turbulent medium that fills the space **between crystal spheres**. It is not Wildspace — it is stranger and more dangerous.

## Properties
- **Wildly flammable** — open flames are forbidden in the Flow. Even sparks can ignite a catastrophic explosion. Magical flames are equally dangerous.
- **Disorienting** — the Flow has no gravity, no up or down, and no stars. Navigation is purely by instrument or experience.
- **Inaccessible to most magic** — spells that contact other planes (Sending, Commune, planar travel) do not function in the Flow.
- **No air** — ships must rely on their own air envelopes; the Flow itself is breathable only within a sealed ship.

## Travel
- Entering the Flow requires passing through the shell of a **crystal sphere** — only spelljamming ships can do this
- Travel time between spheres: **weeks to months** depending on destination and Flow conditions
- Flow currents can speed or slow travel — experienced navigators know the best routes

## Hazards
- **Phlogiston fires** — accidental ignitions; can destroy a ship in seconds
- **Flow pirates** — raiders who lurk in the Flow to ambush travelers between spheres
- **Temporal anomalies** — time passes strangely; travelers sometimes arrive before they expected, or very late
- **Madness** — prolonged exposure to the formless swirling colors can cause Wisdom saving throws (DC 12 per week; failure = short-term madness)
