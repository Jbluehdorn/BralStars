# Travelling in Wildspace

## On the Rock of Bral
Travel on the Rock itself is on foot or by hired wagon. The Rock is roughly a mile long — moving between districts takes 10–30 minutes on foot.

## Spelljamming Travel
To travel in Wildspace, a party needs:
1. A **spelljamming vessel** (bought, rented, or borrowed)
2. A **spelljamming helm** (the magical pilot seat)
3. A pilot with sufficient magical ability (any spellcaster)

### Travel Speeds
- Within the Rock of Bral's immediate vicinity: minutes
- To a nearby asteroid or object: hours
- Across Realmspace to another planet: days to weeks
- Through the Phlogiston to another crystal sphere: weeks to months

## Navigation
- Known routes have charted maps available (for purchase at [[The Grand Bazaar]])
- Unknown regions require Survival or Navigation checks
- See [[Hex Exploration]] for rules on exploring uncharted areas

## Hazards
- **Space monsters** — roll for random encounters on extended journeys
- **Pirates** — more common near trade lanes
- **Gravity anomalies** — near large bodies
- **Phlogiston fires** — the Flow is highly flammable; open flames are forbidden
- **Spelljammer madness** — prolonged time in the Flow can cause Wisdom saving throws
