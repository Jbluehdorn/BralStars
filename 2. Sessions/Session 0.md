# Overview
Session 0 is a session-zero and campaign introduction. No adventuring — just world-building, character creation, and setting expectations.

# The Rock of Bral
- Introduce the setting: **the Rock of Bral**, an asteroid city drifting through Wildspace
- Describe the major districts: Low City, Middle City, High City
- Introduce key factions the players may interact with
- Explain the West Marches format:
  - Players choose their own adventures
  - Multiple crews can operate simultaneously
  - Post jobs/rumors on the **Adventurers' Board** in [[The Laughing Beholder]]

# West Marches Expectations
- Sessions are scheduled by the players, not the DM
- Players must post in the group chat to form a party and pick a goal
- No single "main party" — any combination of characters can go on any adventure
- Session recap must be posted after each session

# Character Creation
- Decide race, class, background
- Pick a reason your character is on the Rock of Bral
- Establish at least one connection to another PC or NPC in the city
- Introduce your character to the group
