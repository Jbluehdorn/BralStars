# Character Creation

Use these guidelines when creating your character for **Bral Stars**.

---

## Sources Allowed

- **Player's Handbook** — all races, classes, backgrounds
- **Spelljammer: Adventures in Space** — strongly recommended for race options (Astral Elves, Autognomes, Giff, Hadozee, Plasmoids, Thri-kreen)
- **Other official sourcebooks** — ask the DM first

---

## Your Character's Hook

Every character needs a reason to be on the Rock of Bral. Pick one or invent your own:

- You arrived on a ship and never left
- You're a native — born and raised in Low City (or Middle, or High City)
- You're running from something on your home world
- You came looking for someone
- You came for the money
- You came because a map led you here
- You were press-ganged onto a ship and the Rock was the first port

---

## Connections

Before your first session, establish:

1. **One connection to another PC** — how do you know them? You don't have to like each other.
2. **One connection to an NPC on the Rock** — a contact, a rival, a creditor, someone who knows your name

---

## Starting Equipment

Use standard class starting equipment from the PHB. Additionally, every character starts with:

- Lodgings on the Rock (Low City bunk, shared room, or better if your background supports it)
- A reason to check the Adventurers' Board
- One rumor about the Rock that may or may not be true

---

## Leveling

Characters gain levels through **milestones**, not XP. The DM will announce when a level-up is earned — typically after a significant adventure or discovery.

---

## Multiclassing & Feats

Both are allowed. Ask the DM if you're unsure whether a combination makes sense for the setting.
