# Factions & Connections

The groups that shape daily life on the Rock. Some are proud of what they do. Some are proud of what they *say* they do. Most are up to something.

Every character should have **at least one connection** to a faction — an ally, a rival, a debt, a history. Pick one below and tell the DM.

---

## The Hammers
**Type:** Neighborhood collective (Robin-Hood-adjacent) · **Base:** The Boroughs · **Leader:** [[Jennifer Coolidge]] (HOA president)

An intersectional community organized to redistribute wealth from the Rock's elites down to the neighborhoods that need it. Sincere. Ideological. **Chronically bogged down in HOA process** — every action ends in a three-hour meeting.

**Connection hooks:** You grew up in the Boroughs · you owe Jennifer a favor · you disagree with the HOA's methods · you're on the redistribution list.

---

## The Cirque
**Type:** Circus troupe · **Historical reputation:** Assassins · **Ringmaster:** [[Ringmaster Blorg]] (a crocodile)

A legitimate circus troupe fighting a well-earned reputation as contract killers. They run the fights at [[Important Locations|the Arena of Frun]] — an open secret that the outcomes are *scripted, WWE-style*. The Cirque is also, to a member, terrified of clowns. Nobody will say why.

**Connection hooks:** Former troupe member · a Cirque assassin killed someone you loved · you know the fights are fixed · you help the Cirque go legit.

---

## The Red Masks
**Type:** Bardic house / information network

Where the Rock's musicians and bards gather to trade songs and information. The finest rumor mill on the Rock. Members have been quietly disappearing — the Masks won't say to where.

**Connection hooks:** You're a member · a Red Mask friend of yours vanished · you owe the Masks a song.

---

## The Groundhogs
**Type:** Silent underground guides · **Vow:** They do not speak

Revered guides who will take you through the tunnels beneath the Rock. Payment by gesture and by weight. Reliability: uneven. Sometimes they just leave you.

**Connection hooks:** A Groundhog once saved your life · a Groundhog once *stranded* you · you're apprenticed to their silent order.

---

## The Lovers of the Lake
**Type:** Small hippie collective · **Base:** [[Important Locations|Lake Bral]]

Peaceful, barefoot, unshakably certain that Lake Bral is a god. Ask them for lunch, get invited to a ritual.

**Connection hooks:** A family member joined · you're a lapsed Lover · the Lake asked you a favor.

---

## Gaspar Reclamations
**Type:** Family salvage yard · **Reputation:** Wrongly rumored to be mob-run

Runs the largest scrap operation in Low City. Insists — correctly — that they're just a family business. Nobody believes them.

**Connection hooks:** You worked there as a kid · you're the reason for a rumor about them · you know what's actually in the back yard.

---

## The Donjon Crime Family
**Type:** Old-money organized crime · **Head:** [[Mafia Don Jon]] · **Base:** The Donjon estate

Rackets, protection, cargo skimming at the docks, and a hand in most schemes the Rock's elites deny existing. Officially: a respectable noble house. Unofficially: run everything.

**Connection hooks:** You owe them money · you refused to work for them · a family member is on their payroll.

---

## Xenotermination Ltd.
**Type:** Mining company · **Front:** Legitimate resource extraction · **CEO:** A very nice, well-intentioned person

A "mining" company working the hollow veins beneath the Rock. Rumors of destroyed sites, missing miners, and vanished species — but the CEO gives such lovely charity speeches that nobody wants to press her on it.

**Connection hooks:** You worked a Xeno site · a friend disappeared on one · you're trying to reach the CEO.

---

## The Secret Service Clowns
**Type:** Rumored secret police · **Base:** Starhaven · **Leader (rumored):** [[Ronald McDonald]]

Officially: they don't exist. Unofficially: everyone knows. Prince Andru's alleged secret police, all clowns, who allegedly make dissidents *disappear* in the hedge maze at Starhaven.

**Connection hooks:** Someone you know went into the maze · you *think* you saw a Clown recruitment · you have a warning nobody wants to hear.

---

## The Adventurers (i.e. you)
Every party member starts with a shared connection: **you all frequent [[The Laughing Beholder]] and check the [[Adventurers' Board]].** Beyond that, connections between PCs are yours to invent — pick a shared history, a debt, or a fight that never got resolved.
