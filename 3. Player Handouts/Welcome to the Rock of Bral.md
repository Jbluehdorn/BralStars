# Welcome to the Rock of Bral

You live on the **Rock of Bral** — a mile-long asteroid drifting through Wildspace. Trading hub, port town, black market, home base. Roughly 25,000 souls from a hundred worlds. Everyone here is trading, hiding, or looking for opportunity. You're in the third category.

---

## The Districts

Three districts stacked bottom-to-top of the asteroid. See [[Maps & Visuals]] for the full maps.

### Low City
The docks, warehouses, and rough taverns. Ships arrive, cargo moves, deals get made away from official eyes.
- **[[The Laughing Beholder]]** — your home base. Owned by [[Large Luigi]]. Neutral ground.
- **The Docks** — port for spelljamming ships.
- **The Rat's Maze** — labyrinthine slum. Don't wander in.
- **The Boroughs** — working-class residential.
- **The Fields** — cemetery / compost yard.
- **The Edge** — a very specific brothel.
- **Gaspar Reclamations** — mom-and-pop scrap yard. Rumored to be mob-run.

### Middle City
Commercial heart. Merchants, craftspeople, temples.
- **The Grand Bazaar** — largest market in Wildspace. Haggling expected.
- **Temple Row** — shrines to gods from a hundred worlds.
- **The Gambler's Den** — cards, dice, worse games.
- **The Arena** (the Arena of Frun) — gladiatorial combat.
- **Mages' Hall** — the Rock's school of magic.
- **Library of Spheres** — the Rock's spelljamming atlas.
- **Royal Theatrical Company** — costume shop.
- **Lake Bral** — a freshwater lake, somehow, inside the Rock.

### High City
The powerful.
- **Starhaven** (formally the Palace of Bral) — Prince Andru's estate.
- **The Noble Quarter** — walled manors, private guards.
- **The Man-o'-War** — high-society restaurant where deals get made.

---

## Wildspace

Airless void between worlds. Ships sail it using magical **spelljamming helms**. Beyond the solar system is the **Phlogiston** — a turbulent multicolored medium between crystal spheres. It's highly flammable. Don't bring an open flame.

---

## What You Know About the Rock

Things Session 0 established as canon — things any character living on the Rock would have picked up:

- **Barter economy by weight.** Coin works. Gems work. A bag of nails works, if the nails weigh right. Every stall has a scale.
- **The Rock is hollow.** Old mining carved out most of the interior.
- **The gravity generator is failing.** People have started falling off — mostly at night, mostly at the edges. Rumor: it's the naval-base workers who maintain the fields who are dropping.
- **There is something very secret at the center of Prince Andru's hedge maze.** Nobody who's gone in has come back to describe it.
- **Old rumor:** the Rock was once an illithid colony. Nobody remembers when.
- **Persistent rumor:** Prince Andru has a secret police made of clowns. Officially, they don't exist.

---

## Who's In Charge

**Prince Andru** rules the Rock from Starhaven. Pragmatic, self-interested, not overtly cruel. His constabulary enforces law in Middle and High City. In Low City, law is more of a suggestion.
