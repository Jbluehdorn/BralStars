# West Marches Player Guide

**Bral Stars** is a **West Marches** campaign. This is different from a standard D&D campaign — read this carefully.

---

## The Core Idea

There is no fixed group. There is no scheduled session. **You make it happen.**

The Rock of Bral is your home base. Wildspace and its asteroids, ruins, and dungeons are out there waiting. You decide where to go, who to bring, and when to play.

---

## How to Start a Session

1. **Post in the group chat** with a goal — *"I want to investigate that derelict ship rumor from the Board"* or *"Who wants to explore the asteroid cluster to the spinward?"*
2. **Other players opt in** — minimum 2 characters, maximum 5
3. **Agree on a date and time** with the DM
4. **Play the session**
5. **Post a recap** afterward — a few sentences about what happened, what you found, what changed

The DM will not chase you down to schedule sessions. That's on you.

---

## Between Sessions

Your character is always safely back on the Rock of Bral when not in a session. They can pursue [[Downtime Activities]] — working, training, carousing, researching, or getting into trouble on their own.

Downtime is handled via a quick message to the DM between sessions.

---

## The World is Persistent

Everything that happens carries forward.

- NPCs remember you
- Locations change after you interact with them  
- Factions track your reputation — good and bad
- Other parties' actions affect the world you explore

If another group clears a dungeon, it's cleared. If they make an enemy of a faction, that faction may be hostile to you too. **Communicate with other players.**

---

## Character Death

Death is possible and permanent. The Rock of Bral has resurrection services, but they are **expensive** (at least 500 gp for *Revivify*, more for higher-level spells). There's no guarantee a cleric of the right level is available.

If your character dies and can't be revived, you may create a new character who arrives on the Rock by the next session.

---

## The Adventurers' Board

The [[Adventurers' Board]] at the Laughing Beholder is the central source of jobs, rumors, and expedition opportunities. Check it regularly — it updates between sessions as the DM adds new hooks.
