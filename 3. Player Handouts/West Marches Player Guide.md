# West Marches Player Guide

**Bral Stars** is a **West Marches** style campaign — an open world with a persistent setting, no fixed main storyline, and player-driven exploration. Here's how it runs.

---

## Schedule

| Event | When | Duration |
|-------|------|----------|
| **Main Session** | 3rd Tuesday of each month | ~3 hours, 5:30 PM PST |
| **Workshop** (optional) | The Thursday following the main session | ~1 hour, 5:30 PM PST |

**Main Sessions** are where the adventure happens — exploration, combat, roleplay, and progressing plot hooks.

**Workshop Thursdays** are optional low-key sessions for downtime, character admin, shopping, NPC conversations, or tidying up loose ends from the previous session. No pressure to attend, but they're a great chance to do the stuff that doesn't need the full table.

---

## Monthly Hook Voting

At the **start of each month**, the DM posts a set of available plot hooks in Discord. Everyone votes on which hook the group wants to pursue that month's session.

- Hooks range from combat-heavy to investigation to exploration
- Votes are posted in Discord — check the pinned messages
- The winning hook becomes the focus of that month's session
- Unvoted hooks don't disappear — they stay available for future months

You're never locked out of pursuing something off the board if your party has a strong lead to follow, but the voted hook takes priority for the scheduled session.

---

## Between Sessions

Your character is always safely back on the Rock of Bral when not in a session. Between sessions you can:

- Pursue [[Downtime Activities]] — send a message to the DM with what you're doing
- Post questions or plans in Discord
- Check the [[Adventurers' Board]] for updated rumors and jobs

---

## The World is Persistent

Everything that happens carries forward.

- NPCs remember you
- Locations change after you interact with them
- Factions track your reputation — good and bad
- What the party does in one session shapes the world for the next

---

## Character Death

Death is possible and permanent. The Rock of Bral has resurrection services, but they are **expensive** (at least 500 gp for *Revivify*, more for higher-level spells). There's no guarantee a cleric of the right level is available.

If your character dies and can't be revived, you may create a new character who arrives on the Rock by the next session.
